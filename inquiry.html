01 / 10 Refined Intake 精简采集
01
May we have your name? 请问如何称呼您？
A simple beginning. 先从最基本的信息开始。
02
How should we reach you? 我们该如何与您联系？
Email, WeChat, or another preferred contact. 请填写 Email、微信或您偏好的联系方式。
03
Where will your journey begin? 您的出发城市是？
04
And where are you headed? 您的目的地是？
05
When would you ideally like to travel? 您理想中的出行时间是？
An exact date is not necessary — a window is enough. 不一定要非常精确，时间范围即可。
06
What kind of itinerary are you considering? 您目前考虑的是哪种行程形式？
07
Which best describes this trip? 以下哪项最符合这次出行情况？
08
What matters most for this journey? 这次行程中，您最看重什么？
09
What would you most like us to reduce? 您最希望我们帮您降低哪方面的不确定性？
10
Is there anything else we should quietly keep in mind? 还有什么偏好或顾虑，是您希望我们提前纳入考虑的？
Family preferences, baggage, routing dislikes, timing sensitivities, or anything that would make the journey feel more effortless. 例如家人偏好、行李情况、不喜欢的转机方式、时间敏感点，或任何能让旅程更省心的信息。
Your details will be used only to understand your travel needs. 这些信息仅用于帮助我们理解您的出行需求。
Thank you. Your brief has been received. 感谢提交，我们已经收到您的信息。
